QUICK START

Build your way to 10 reputation.

  1. 01
    Choose one action, then pass the device.

    On your turn, draft one Time & Talent card or complete one Order & Milestone brief. Play then moves to the next founder.

  2. 02
    Start with a founder strength.

    Every company gets a named founder and +1 permanent skill. Talent cards add Brand, Fulfillment, Production, Staffing, and Operations.

  3. 03
    Trade cards—or outsource—for growth.

    Used cards are discarded. While completing a brief, pay $3 per outsourced skill point. Rewards grant reputation, cash, or permanent strengths.

  4. 04
    Keep the market moving.

    Whenever you take a card, the row slides right and a fresh card enters on the left.

  5. 05
    Use cash tactically.

    Pay $1 to refresh only the talent market without using your turn. Open briefs stay in place. Or invest $5 for 1 reputation.

  6. 06
    Expect chaos—if you invited it.

    A Chaos Monkey can appear while refilling the talent market. Resolve it immediately; ongoing effects last until the next monkey appears.
